Rules PC

Racing Rules

Sprint Round:

  • Qualifying will run for 5 minutes.
  • Each race will have its own qualifying.
  • Each round will have 2x 35-minute races.
  • Each race will require 1 pitstop with fuel taken, tyres optional.
  • Each race will have its own points, awarded as detailed below.

Endurance:

  • Qualifying will run for 10 minutes.
  • Each round will have a single 75-minute race.
  • Each race will require 1 pitstop with fuel taken, tyres optional.
  • Endurance rounds will receive a 150% points bonus.

General Rules:

  • Qualifying sessions will start at 19:45 UK / 20:45 CET
  • Servers will be reset at approximately 19:00 UK / 20:00 CET for free practice.
  • Each track we visit is a round. Points will be scored for each race at each round. I.E. Sprint round is 25 points to each overall race winner and so on based on finishing position.
  • Changes in car will result in loss of all championship points gained up to the change in car, for both individual and teams championship

  • Use realistic names instead of nicknames or usernames in our league. This will make the experience more authentic and enjoyable

  • A driver must finish the race to be awarded points.
  • A driver must be signed up to the championship to earn points
